AMS Domain Reference (loaded into the system prompt)

Modules
-------
- Agent Profile        : registration, basic data, hierarchy, team
- License Management   : issue, renew, suspend, complaints, sanctions
- Product Authorization: product-by-agent matrix, training prerequisites
- Commission Engine    : calculation, tiers, allocation, team commission
- Performance Tracking : production, KPIs, ranking, incentives
- Customer Assignment  : assignment, relationship management, transfer
- Compliance Monitor   : licence renewal alerts, CPD tracking, regulatory reports

Hierarchy
---------
Agent  →  Unit Manager (UM)  →  Branch Manager (BM)  →  Regional Director (RD)
Override commission flows upward; persistency and production gates apply.

Commission Lifecycle
--------------------
earned → accrued → clawback window → released → paid
- Year-1 life: typical 30-55% rate, full clawback if lapsed
- Year-2 life: 10-20% rate, partial clawback
- Year-3+ life: 3-10% tail; some products zero out by year 6
- Health/property/motor/travel: usually flat-rate, annually renewable

Compliance Pillars
------------------
- Data privacy        : HK PDPO, SG PDPA, EU GDPR, US state privacy laws
- Commission disclosure: HK IA GL20, EU IDD Art. 19, MAS FAA-N03, NAIC #275
- Agent licensing     : HK Insurance Ordinance Cap 41, SG MAS FAA, NAIC PLMA
- Consumer protection : cooling-off period (HK GL21: 21 days for long-term)
- Reporting           : IFRS 17, Solvency II for solvency-regulated insurers

PII Sensitivity
---------------
HKID, NRIC, SSN, passport, policy number, bank account, credit card, IBAN,
medical history (PHI). Mask in logs; encrypt at rest; tokenise where feasible.

Performance KPIs
----------------
APE, VNB, Persistency 13M / 25M, conversion rate, average policy size,
commission per agent / per FTE.

Audit Trail
-----------
All commission formula changes, licence overrides, customer reassignments
must record actor, timestamp, before/after value, reason, approver.
Retention: HK 7y, SG 5y, UK 6y, US life 10y.
